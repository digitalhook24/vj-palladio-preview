About VJ Palladio Kothrud Central

VJ Palladio Kothrud Central is a contemporary residential landmark at Pratik Nagar, Gujrat Colony, Kothrud, Pune. Spread across a 3.5-acre land parcel, the project features 4 artistic G+27 towers offering luxurious 3 BHK premium residences amidst lush greenery. Curated for limitless urban living, it delivers 50+ premium amenities including a grand clubhouse, swimming pool, open gymnasium, jogging track, kids' play area, landscaped gardens, amphitheatre, multipurpose court, acupressure pathway, temple and more. With possession scheduled for December 2028 and instant on-spot booking benefits for the first 100 customers, it is one of Kothrud's most awaited new-launch addresses.
