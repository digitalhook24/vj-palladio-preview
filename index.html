Welcome to VJ Palladio Presidential Suite
Balewadi, Pune

VJ Palladio Presidential Suite Balewadi, Pune, is a premium residential project offering thoughtfully designed homes in one of the city’s most vibrant and well-connected neighbourhoods. Located near Balewadi High Street, Hinjawadi IT Park, Baner, and major lifestyle hubs, the development ensures seamless access to top employment centres, schools, hospitals, and retail destinations. The residences feature contemporary layouts, elegant finishes, and abundant natural light to elevate everyday living. Residents enjoy lifestyle amenities such as landscaped gardens, a clubhouse, fitness zones, children’s play areas, and multipurpose community spaces. Palladio Balewadi Central 2 blends urban convenience, comfort, and value for modern families.
