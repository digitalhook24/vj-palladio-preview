About VJ Indiwork Kothrud Central

VJ Indiwork Kothrud Central is a modern commercial destination in the heart of Kothrud, Pune, combining sleek contemporary architecture with efficient, high-performance workspaces. Designed for entrepreneurs, professionals and growing businesses, it offers optimised 265 – 355 sq.ft. boutique office spaces that deliver lower capital entry and higher utilisation value. Strategically connected to Pune International Airport, Pune Railway Station and the Mumbai – Bangalore Highway, and supported by workspace amenities like a co-working zone, gymnasium, cafeteria, party hall, salon, fast internet, power backup and ample car parking, VJ Indiwork is built for strong rental yield, self-use and a projected assured ROI of 8%.
